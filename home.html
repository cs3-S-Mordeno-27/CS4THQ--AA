What is Matter?

Matter is anything that has mass and takes up space. It is made up of atoms and molecules and can exist in different states such as solid, liquid, gas, and plasma. The properties of matter can be divided into physical and chemical properties.

Solid

A solid is characterized by particles that are closely packed together in a fixed arrangement.

Liquid

A liquid takes the shape of its container but maintains a constant volume independent of pressure.

Gas

A gas has no fixed shape or volume and will expand to fill the space available to it.

Plasma

Plasma is a state of matter similar to gas in which a certain portion of the particles are ionized.
